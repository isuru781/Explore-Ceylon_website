About Explore Ceylon

Explore Ceylon is dedicated to providing you with authentic and enriching travel experiences in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to showcase the island's diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality. We curate unique itineraries that cater to your interests, ensuring an unforgettable journey.

Key Features

Report scam and view scams

Stay Safe with Real-time Scam Alerts!
Our platform empowers travelers by allowing them to report scams and view user-submitted scam alerts in real-time. Stay informed, avoid tourist traps, and explore Sri Lanka with confidence.

Event Alert system

Never Miss an Event in Sri Lanka!
Event Alert System keeps you updated on the latest cultural festivals, concerts, and special events happening across Sri Lanka. Discover hidden events in your area, plan your travels better, and experience the vibrant culture like never before

Intelligent Assistance via Chatbo

24/7 Smart Travel Assistance!
Our Intelligent Assistance via Chatbot is here to help you anytime, anywhere. Whether you need travel tips, booking assistance, or quick answers to your questions, our AI-powered chatbot ensures a seamless and stress-free travel experience in Sri Lanka.
